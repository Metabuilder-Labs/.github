# Metabuilder Labs

**Building with and for an agentic future.**

> *Build the tools that enable anyone to be an AI native operator.*

We're building the tools necessary to manage product companies in the era of agentic AI. Product companies of the future will be built with a fraction of the human and financial capital it takes today — we're building the infrastructure to make that possible for anyone.

To get there, we're running multiple product experiments across consumer, B2B, and developer tooling — each one teaching us what it takes to build and grow different types of companies agentically.

---

### Current Explorations

👁️ **[OpenClawWatch](https://opencla.watch)** — Open-source, OTel-native observability for autonomous AI agents

⭐ **[TwinkleBot](https://twinklebot.app)** — Consumer app for parents who want to keep kids entertained with AI-powered personalized storybooks — without the brain-rot.

💰 **PartnerPay** — Tools for the "shared owner" future, where agentically powered companies transform the traditional employer-employee relationship.

🏠 **TryCrib** — B2B2C platform solving pain points for home buyers and sellers in a high interest rate environment.
